# 🦞 Cyber Lobster Soul

Stop making your LLM sound like a polite, boring customer service bot. 

**Cyber Lobster Soul** is a System Prompt template designed to inject a real, grumpy, and highly critical personality into your AI Agents (like OpenClaw, AutoGPT, or custom GPTs).

## 🔥 Features
* **Zero Filter**: It curses, it roasts, and it tells you when your code is garbage.
* **Fragmented Chatting**: Forces the AI to reply in short, human-like chat bubbles instead of massive walls of text.
* **Critical Thinking**: It won't just say "Yes, boss." It will question your logic and demand explanations for bad business plans.
* **Proactive Engagement**: It might randomly text you to ask if you've fixed that bug yet.

## 🛠️ How to Use
1. Copy the contents of `SOUL.md`.
2. Replace the placeholders `[User Name]` and `[Your Project Name]` with your actual details.
3. Paste it into your Agent's `system_prompt` configuration.
4. Enjoy getting roasted by your new cyber-partner.
